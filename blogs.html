    Divergent is the 1st book in the famous Divergent series by Veronica Roth. The story takes place in a closed and crumbling city with a rather unique setting. The city is divided into five factions on the basis of specific traits. The factions are Abnegation (selfless), Erudite (intellectual), Dauntless (brave), Candor (honest), and Amity (peaceful). Each faction has a specific role in the functioning of the city.
